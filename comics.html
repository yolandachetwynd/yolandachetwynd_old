Invasion, a Graphic Novel

Yolanda Chetwynd has written a graphic novel, which takes about ten minutes to read. It includes the story of her mother's birth in 1940, as told to Yolanda by her grandmother in 1987. You can download a pdf* of this work by clicking here.

You can listen to Yolanda Chetwynd speak about her graphic novel in a Delaware State of the Arts Podcast broadcast May 4, 2014: http://www.artsdel.org/podcasts/140504_de_state_of_arts.mp3

If you have more time and would like to explore the background information to Yolanda's family story, here is the index of supplementary documents.

Here is Yolanda's unabridged draft version of her graphic novel about her family's survival during the Second World War, and how they traveled across three continents. Gentien the Corporal is 108 pages long.
