Yoyoma Comic

In the summer of 2008 I had just competed my first "Body for Life Challenge." The transformation so impressed my nephew, Oliver Cairns, that he draw a comic story for and about me. He was eleven at the time. I, in turn, was inspired by the superheroine costume and the story that I redrew  and edited it. I hope reading it will inspire you to become a superhero of your own.
